Italy recently signed a deal with Albania allowing it to build refugee centers in Albanian territory. Albania would surrender sovereignty in those areas and they’d be used to house migrants trying to get to Italy. It’s similar to what the Uk tried with Rwanda, Australia with Nauru, and the us historically with Guantanamo bay. Rich countries using poor ones to house migrants. I understand the logic behind it. But it reeks of colonialism, especially if the poorer country is semi-autocratic
